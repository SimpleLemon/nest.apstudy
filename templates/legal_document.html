4. Cookie Policy

4.1 What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit, which can make your next visit easier and the site more useful to you.

4.2 Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential/Session Cookies Required for login, authentication, and maintaining your session. Without these, the Service cannot function properly. Expire when you close your browser or after your session ends.
Authentication Cookies Remember that you are logged in so you do not have to sign in on every page or visit. Persist until you log out or the session expires, typically 30 days.
Preference Cookies Store your settings such as theme, layout preferences, and other customization choices. Persist until you clear them or change your preferences.
Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics) Distinguish unique users, track session information, and record how you interact with the Service. _ga: 2 years; _ga_*: 2 years; _gid: 24 hours.

4.3 Third-Party Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies on your device to collect usage data. These cookies are governed by Google's privacy policy. We do not allow any other third-party advertising or tracking cookies on the Service.

4.4 Managing Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies through their settings. Blocking essential cookies will prevent the Service from functioning properly.
  • Google Analytics Opt-Out: Install the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.
  • Do Not Track: We respect your browser's "Do Not Track" (DNT) signal. If your browser sends a DNT signal, we will not load analytics cookies.

Please be aware that disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of the Service.
